# docs/

Written deliverables and the figures that support them.

What belongs here:

- The approved proposal, which is the source of truth for scope.
- The strategic AI roadmap: short/medium/long-term adoption recommendations,
  feasibility, risks, data gaps.
- Drafts of the ethics and governance write-ups.
- Figures exported from the notebooks in `model/`.

Prose conventions, from CLAUDE.md: direct, lightly hedged, no marketing language, no
em-dashes. Concrete examples over abstract framing. Refer to the dataset sponsor as
"a national veterans nonprofit" rather than by name.

Plots: no pastel palettes, no chart junk, truthful axes.
